How do I find my routing and account numbers?

Select the "Accounts" tab from the side navigation bar. Then choose the account you are looking for and select the blue "Details" button on the far right. From here you should see both your account number and routing number.

How do I report suspected fraud?

From any page on the site, navigate to the top left of the screen. Here you will see two buttons, "Log Out" and "Contact Us". Select "Contact Us". Fill out the form and mention suspected fraud in the subject of the message. One of our team members will reach out to you within 3 business days to work on resolving the issue.

How do I create a new password or find my email?

Select the "Profile" tab from the side navigation bar. Here you will see two headers, "My Info" and "Security". Under "My Info" is where you will find your associated email and phone number. Under "Security" if you would like to change your password you must first enter your old password. After entering your old password simply enter the new password you would like to use and then confirm that password, then select "Submit" and your password will be updated.

How do I view my accounts credit score?

Select the "Accounts" tab from the side navigation bar. Then choose the account you are looking for and select the blue "Details" button on the far right. Just below the Account Summary you will see the "Credit Score" header, here you will find the numerical value of your credit score inside of a graph that represents the numerical value.
